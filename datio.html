Step 01 : Initiating Item Addition

To start adding a new item, go to the home screen within the app and select the '+ ADD ITEM' option.

----------------------------------------

Step 02 : Filling in Item Details

When you choose to add an item, an item entry window will be displayed. Here's how you can categorize and fill in the details:

Required Fields

  • Item Name - Enter the name of the item
  • Price - Specify the price of the item

Optional Fields

  • Barcode - If available, you can enter the item's barcode. Users of the Datio POS base station have the added convenience of scanning the item to automatically fill in this field
  • Seq ID - Use this field to arrange the order of menu items on the home page.
  • Color Tag - Assign a color tag to the item for easy identification or categorization.
  • Add Taxes - Specify whether the item is subject to VAT at checkout or if it is exempt from VAT and other taxes
  • Measure by Unit - Check this option if the item is quantified by a specific unit (e.g., grams)
  • Prompt for Price - Select this box if you want to add generic items like 'Drinks,' 'Beer,' or 'Chips,' and the system will prompt for the price upon selection. For stores with a large inventory who prefer not to add each item individually, our bulk upload feature is ideal. Simply send us your spreadsheet, and we'll handle the addition of thousands of items for you.
  • Gift Card - Select this box to categorize the new item as a Gift Card for Sale.
----------------------------------------

Step 03 : Modifiers

In Datio POS, item modifiers allow you to add options or variations to a product, enhancing its appeal and functionality. For example, when selling a piece of apparel, you can introduce modifiers for size, color, or material.

To incorporate these modifications

  1. Begin by selecting your product, then access the 'Modifiers' option from the item menu.
  2. To open the item menu, either click on 'ADD ITEM' or press and hold an existing product to enter edit mode.
  3. Add your desired modifiers, such as 'Size: S, M, L, XL', 'Color: Red, Blue, Green', or 'Material: Cotton, Polyester'. These modifiers can easily customize your product offering.
  4. Enable notes for special requests or instructions, like 'Gift wrap' or 'Include a greeting card'.
  5. Modifiers can either adjust the product's price or be set at no additional cost ($0.00).

This feature is tailored to cater to the diverse needs of retail operations, allowing for an enhanced and personalized shopping experience

----------------------------------------

When you select an item for sale, 'Blue Shirt' in this example, a pop-up will prompt you to specify the size modifier, such as 'S', 'M', 'L' or 'XL'. This ensures the exact variant is selected according to the customer's preference

----------------------------------------

The cart will display the selected modifiers, and the total price will adjust to include the cost of these modifiers. Both the notes and modifiers will be printed on the customer's receipt.

----------------------------------------

Step 04 : Quantity

The 'Quantity' section in the Item Window is designed for comprehensive inventory management:

  • Item Cost - Enter your purchase cost for the item, enabling the system to automatically calculate your profit margins
  • Quantity Available - Specify how many units of each item you currently have in stock
  • Restock Threshold - Set the inventory level that triggers a reminder to reorder the item
  • Receive SMS/Email Alerts - Enable notifications to be sent when inventory falls below the Restock Threshold, reminding you it's time to reorder.
  • Supplier Name - Record the name of the supplier for each item, making it easier to know whom to contact for restocking.
  • Supplier Phone Number - Keep your supplier's contact number handy for easy communication when it's time to replenish your stock
----------------------------------------

Step 05 : Completing the Setup

After entering all the necessary information, you will be presented with a few options:

  • Add the item to the cart and save it
  • Save the item details without adding it to the cart
  • Add the item to the cart without saving the details
----------------------------------------

Note: When editing an existing item, the 'Add to Cart' button will be inactive. To add an item to the cart, use the Item Window Pop-Up for first-time auditions. For subsequent additions, simply select from the items displayed on your home page menu.Now let's get selling!

----------------------------------------

Step 06 : Understanding and Using Colored Dots for Item Categorization

  • The colored dots located on the left panel serve as a straightforward visual tool for categorizing items within your inventory. By assigning a specific color to different categories of items, you can organize your offerings more efficiently
  • Selecting a colored dot filters the displayed items on the home page menu to only those assigned to the chosen color category. For broader search criteria, you have the option to select multiple colors simultaneously, allowing the system to display items that match any of the selected color categories
  • In the example provided, selecting both purple and gold colors results in the menu displaying only items that belong to these categories

This streamlined process ensures that you can efficiently manage your inventory and sales directly from the X POS App. Now let's get selling
