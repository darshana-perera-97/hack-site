Tech Innovators 2024

About the Event

Join us at Tech Innovators 2024, a premier hackathon event designed to bring together creative minds, tech enthusiasts, and developers. Whether you're a student, professional, or hobbyist, this hackathon offers a platform to showcase your skills, collaborate with others, and bring innovative ideas to life. Participants will work in teams to tackle challenges in various themes, including Smart Cities & IoT, Health Tech, FinTech, and Sustainable Development. The event aims to foster innovation, provide networking opportunities, and award top-performing teams with exciting prizes.

Themes & Challenges

Dive into the exciting realms of Generative AI, IoT, and Telco Applications.
Your challenge is to identify a problem within an existing system and develop a creative solution, or introduce a novel approach that enhances the efficiency and usability of current processes. Whether you aim to solve a specific issue or revolutionize the way things are done, this is your opportunity to innovate and make a significant impact.

Prizes

Every participant will receive a valuable certificate of participation, and a physical award ceremony will be held to honor the winners.
